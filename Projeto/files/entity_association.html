Entity-Association Diagram · Firebase Firestore · Lucas Alemão Nº 15052 · ENIDH · Computação Móvel 2025/2026

Architecture: Two-level database — shared (vessels collection, visible to all users) and private (each user's history, voyages, saved vessels). First user to search a vessel by IMO populates data for all future users.

Firestore Collection Schema

Firestore (root)
├── vessels/{imo}                   [SHARED — all users]
│   ├── imo, name, type
│   ├── loa, beam, deadweight
│   ├── lightshipWeight, lightshipKG
│   ├── numberOfHolds, flag
│   ├── isEstimated (boolean)
│   ├── registeredBy (uid)
│   └── lastUpdated (timestamp)
│
└── users/{uid}                     [PRIVATE — per user]
    ├── profile/
    │   ├── displayName, email
    │   ├── plan ("free" | "pro")
    │   └── createdAt
    ├── searchHistory/{id}/
    │   ├── imo, vesselName
    │   └── searchedAt
    ├── savedVessels/{imo}/
    │   ├── imo, vesselName
    │   └── savedAt
    └── voyages/{id}/               [PRO only]
        ├── vesselImo, vesselName
        ├── departurePort, arrivalPort
        ├── gmResult, kgResult, isStable
        ├── totalCargo, cargoData (map)
        └── createdAt
