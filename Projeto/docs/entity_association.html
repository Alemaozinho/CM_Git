Firestore Collection Schema

Firestore (root)
├── vessels/{imo}                     [SHARED — all users read/write]
│   ├── imo, name, type
│   ├── loa, beam, deadweight
│   ├── lightshipWeight, lightshipKG
│   ├── numberOfHolds, flag
│   ├── isEstimated (boolean)
│   ├── registeredBy (uid FK)
│   └── lastUpdated (timestamp)
│
└── users/{uid}                       [PRIVATE — per authenticated user]
    ├── profile/
    │   ├── displayName, email
    │   ├── plan  ("free" | "pro")
    │   └── createdAt
    ├── searchHistory/{id}/
    │   ├── imo, vesselName
    │   └── searchedAt
    ├── savedVessels/{imo}/
    │   ├── imo, vesselName
    │   └── savedAt
    └── voyages/{id}/                 [PRO only]
        ├── vesselImo, vesselName
        ├── departurePort, arrivalPort
        ├── gmResult, kgResult, isStable
        ├── totalCargo
        ├── cargoData (map: hold → weight)
        └── createdAt
